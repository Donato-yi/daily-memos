## What I Learned Today
- Review tooling gets much more useful when it shows run shape, not just pass/fail or a single score.
- Approval rate, failure density, and longest-step visibility make it easier to tell whether a workflow needs prompt fixes, tool redesign, or staging changes.
- Governance and customization are becoming core parts of agent tooling, not optional enterprise add-ons.
